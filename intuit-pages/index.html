Blue Rock Procurement

Desktop procurement automation, built by Mindsheep

Blue Rock Procurement is a desktop application (macOS and Windows) built by Mindsheep for Blue Rock for General Trading Ltd. It automates the intake of purchase and service request documents, extracts their contents for human review, and records approved entries as bills in the company's QuickBooks Online account, with the original source document attached.

The application is private, internal-use software. It is not distributed publicly and is not listed on the Intuit App Store. It runs entirely on the customer's own computers; there is no hosted service component.

Connection to QuickBooks Online is made through Intuit's OAuth 2.0 authorization flow and can only be established by an authorized administrator of the QuickBooks company. The connection can be revoked at any time — see disconnecting the app.
