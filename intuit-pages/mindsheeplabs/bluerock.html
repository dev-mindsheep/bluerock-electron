About the App

Blue Rock Procurement is a desktop application built by Mindsheep Labs exclusively for Blue Rock. It automates the intake of procurement documents (purchase and service requests), extracts their contents for human review, and records the reviewed results as bills in Blue Rock's QuickBooks Online company, with the original source document attached to each bill.

This is a private, internal tool. It is not commercially distributed, publicly available, or listed on the QuickBooks App Store. It is installed directly on Blue Rock's own computers.

Getting Started

The application is supplied and installed by Mindsheep Labs as part of the engagement with Blue Rock. There is no public download. Once installed, an administrator of the QuickBooks Online company connects the app via Settings → QuickBooks → Connect, which uses Intuit's standard OAuth 2.0 sign-in.
