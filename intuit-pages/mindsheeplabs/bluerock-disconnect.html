Disconnecting from QuickBooks

Blue Rock QuickBooks Desktop Integration

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

What Disconnecting Does

The QuickBooks Online connection used by the App has been disconnected, or you are reviewing how to disconnect it.

When the connection is ended — either from within the App's Settings, or from Intuit's side via My Apps in QuickBooks Online — the App's access tokens are invalidated and it can no longer read from or write to the QuickBooks company. Bills and attachments already created remain in QuickBooks; nothing is deleted by disconnecting.

Local Data

Documents and extracted data stored locally on Blue Rock's computers are not affected by disconnecting. They can be removed at any time by deleting the App's data folder or uninstalling the App.

Reconnecting

To reconnect, open the App and go to Settings → QuickBooks → Connect, then sign in as an administrator of the QuickBooks company.
