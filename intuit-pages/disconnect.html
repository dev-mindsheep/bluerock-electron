Disconnecting QuickBooks from Blue Rock Procurement

The QuickBooks Online connection used by Blue Rock Procurement has been disconnected, or you are reviewing how to disconnect it.

When the connection is disconnected — either from within the application's Settings, or from Intuit's side via My Apps in QuickBooks Online — the application's access tokens are invalidated and it can no longer read from or write to the QuickBooks company. Bills and attachments already created remain in QuickBooks; nothing is deleted by disconnecting.

Documents and extracted data stored locally on your computer are not affected. You can remove them at any time by deleting the application's data folder or uninstalling the application.

To reconnect, open Blue Rock Procurement → Settings → QuickBooks → Connect, and sign in as an administrator of the QuickBooks company.
